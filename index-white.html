CamelProject

CamelProject is a collection of useful software.

Secure. CamelProject is safe. We do not share information about our users with anyone. Moreover, we don't even collect it. Nobody knows anything about you.

Free. Our products are completely free. The CamelProject is not a profitable organization.

Cross-platform. CamelProject products are cross-platform. Supported platforms: Android, Windows, iOS, macOS, Linux.

Support service. There is a support service (if you can call it that), which will answer you in the next 24 hours. Just write to the creator of Camel Project and he will answer you.You can also order a website, program or programming training from him.

Simple design. Simple, intuitive design in products from us. You will not get lost, you will not think where to press. But if you have any problems - write to the support service.

No bugs. No bugs were found during testing. If so, write to the support service.

Gratitude. Thanks to Yandex for the share block, Atabey Gusenli for translation into Turkish and Azerbaijani. GitHub for the free domain and also Vectto for the CamelProject logo.
