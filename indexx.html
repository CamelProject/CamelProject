CamelProject. New Era.

Free. Confidential. Open-source. That's what CP software is.

Secure. CamelProject's first concern is the safety of our users. We put a lot of emphasis on confidentiality, privacy. CamelProject will never disclose our users' data. No one. Not even the authorities and the police. We have created a large selection of really safe, confidential services. This is CamelPrivacy.

Free. The Camel Project will always be completely free. At the moment CP is serviced by volunteers and all costs are borne by the founder. Read more about the team in the «Gratitudes» section

Cross-platform. The CP software is almost entirely cross-platform. We make software for Windows, Linux, Web, Android, we do not release products for Apple operating systems, as it is very expensive. Read more in the «CP and Apple» section.

Support service. We usually respond within an hour, maximum 24 hours. Email us at camelproject@yandex.com or our Telegram bot.

Simple design. We try to make a good design. We have two types of programs: console programs for programmers and graphical programs for users.

No bugs. We test our software well. We have quite few bugs. But if you find any bugs, don't hesitate to write to us.

Support service

If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact our CamelProject Support Team. Our support service is so far only available in English and Russian. You can contact them via e-mail camelproject@yandex.com or via Telegram bot @CamelProjectBot. We answer within 1-24 hours. There is a table of topics of the letter below, if you do not write it we still accept your request, but better help us and write it.

Help CamelProject

CamelProject needs the help of volunteers. It is volunteers, this is because CP is not a commercial organization, it does not earn anything. We spend a lot on servers and other things. That's why we don't have money left over for employee salaries. CP employs only volunteers. If you want to become a volunteer, write to the Helpdesk (see more in Helpdesk section) and write Help or Help RU in the subject line of your email/message. Below you can see what kind of specialists CP needs.

[1] - We accept translators in any language, even unrecognized languages (e.g., Ingush). You can also be a translator for languages that are already in CamelProject (for example, Russian or Azeri).In this case, you will be an assistant to the main translator.

[2] - We need developers who can program in these languages: JavaScript, Python, C++, Assembler FASM. And even those who can only program in the hypertext markup language HTML/CSS.

Specialty Description
Translator Translator for any language [1].
Developer Programmer-developer. We take both proffesionals and beginners. [2]
SEO-Specialist You need to bring the site to the top of search engines and add popularity.
Tester Before a public release, we need to test our software. It is not worth it to mess around with CamelInsider.
